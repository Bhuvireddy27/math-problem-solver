Mean

The mean is the average of a set of numbers. It is calculated by adding all the numbers together and dividing by their count.

Median

The median is the middle value in a set of numbers when arranged in order. If the set has an even count, it is the average of the two middle numbers.

Standard Deviation

The standard deviation measures how spread out numbers are in a dataset. Low standard deviation means the numbers are close to the mean.

Variance

The variance is the average of the squared differences from the mean. It tells how much the values differ from the mean.
